#  CONTRIBUTIONS  How I'll Give Back

This document explains the ways I'll contribute to open-source, research, and the community in 2026.

## Open-source contributions

- Target repos:
  - Hugging Face Transformers / Datasets
  - LangChain
  - FAISS integrations
  - Small community tools (open-source dashboards, evaluation tools)

- Contribution types:
  - Bug fixes and small features
  - Example notebooks and templates
  - Documentation and tutorials

## Writing & Teaching

- Publish blog posts and tutorials (Medium / Dev.to / personal blog)
- Write reproducible example notebooks with clear explanations
- Record short walkthrough videos for key projects

## Community & Talks

- Run or co-run 1-2 workshops / meetups on practical LLMOps and RAG
- Submit talks to local meetups and online conferences

## Research & Experiments

- Share reproducible experiments and model cards
- Contribute to open datasets and evaluation scripts

## How you can collaborate

- Open an issue or a discussion for ideas
- Submit PRs for improvements (follow `CONTRIBUTING.md`)
- Join a working group to co-develop a project
